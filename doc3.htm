An exercise from Module 2

I have choosen Exercise 5 'Developing web authoring skills' as I feel I got the most out of this exercise.

Objectives;

  • The group were to produce a number of web pages, instructions on content given.
  • Individuals were to contribute to the development of the first page and one of the other pages.
In completing this Activity I switched from using the method in Module 1 (Wordpad) as an authoring tool to using Netscape Composer. Using a Composer such as Netscape has definite advantages over Wordpad that I have only brushed on, and will continue to explore, in order to become a more accomplised web author.

This activity encouraged the sharing of tasks within the group and I took an active role in promoting this (see email extract).  I had become one of the more active members of the group, and have gained confidence in communicating and partaking in activities on-line.  This is something I intend to explore further by partaking in other on-line activities (outside of this course and possibly at work).

In compiling my individual page I investigated different versions of an event, obtaining a clearer understanding of the principles of 'clear thinking'.  From this I have become more careful in searching the net and evaluating information obtained, ensuring to obtain information from several sources where possible before drawing conclusions, or using the information, but development of this can only come from continued searching experiences, which I will continue to do.
