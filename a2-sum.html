Summary of Assignment 02 (TMA02)

Part 1- Creating an individual page for a group web

This assignment will be linked to the work that you do as a group in the Tutor Group Activity: 'Creating a web channel'.

You should produce an individual web page covering a particular subject from those listed below which contains, as a minimum:

  • A set of links to 5 web sites relevant to your subject.
  • For each link, a paragraph or two reviewing that site so that the audience will know what to expect if they go to it. (You should apply the 'clear thinking' principles of including the perspective of the sources, indicating the context in which ideas apply and separating observations from theories.)
  • Marks will be given for the general presentation and technical aspects of your individual page (eg: links should work).
The subject areas
Your page should be in one of the subject areas given below. Within the broad subject area you should choose some issue or question on which to focus and then try to find links to pages which answer that question or illuminate that issue. You are free to choose this focus to suit your own interests.
  • Health and disease
  • Current affairs
  • Environment
  • Science and technology
  • Music
  • Arts and culture
  • Sport
(some ideas where given on possible topics within these subject areas)

Templates
Templates where provided for enhancement.

Copyright issues
Guidelines were given on copyright issues, the main points were:

  • In general, you cannot incorporate material from other sources in your own work without first receiving permission from the copyright holder.
  • As a student you may use copyright material for your own personal study purposes only. If you later want to use the same material for any other purpose, you must seek permission.
  • Wherever you include a picture from another site, you should include a caption acknowledging the source (the URL) and date accessed.
  • If you quote directly from another site, another student or any other source, you must use quotation marks and acknowledge the source (for a web site, also include the date accessed).


Part 2 - Reflecting on Module 1

In part 2 of this assignment your task is to produce a word processor document in which you reflect on what you have learned about:

  • Computing with confidence in Sections 3 and 4
  • The groupwork involved in creating your web channel in part 1 of this TMA.
Part 2(a) should be no more than 500 words, and in part 2(b) items (i)-(v) should be no more than 400 words in total.

(a) Computing with confidence

Imagine that you are writing to a friend, or a colleague, who is just beginning to use computers and computer communications. This friend has asked you for advice on key aspects of computing with confidence. In particular, your friend has asked if you could do the following:

(i) Explain your own experiences in learning to use your computer and one or more of the new software packages you learned in Sections 3 or 4. If none of these were new to you, explain how you developed your existing skills further. Give specific examples of what was easy or difficult to learn, where you got stuck and how you overcame this. Illustrate your explanation with samples of working from the specific package.

(ii) Explain the key principles or techniques you have found which help in learning about computers. This should demonstrate your 'learning to learn' skills. It should explain how, other than being shown specific commands and key-presses, you can increase your computing abilities.

(b) The groupwork in your web channel.

This assignment requires you to reflect and comment on the group work that you carried out in the Tutor Group Activity: 'Creating a web channel', to show you have understood some of the issues involved in working in a group.

Your group web channel you created was an opportunity to apply what you learned about groupwork online in Section 2. It may or may not have been particularly successful. Write a paragraph on each of the following:

(i) Describe the way your group worked in producing its web channel.

(ii) Describe ways in which the principles you learned in the readings in Section 2 either were applied or were not.

(iii) Did the group help individuals to produce better work than they could have individually?

(iv) Were there any conflicts or problems in reaching consensus, and if so, how were they resolved?

(v) Did all the members of the group put in sufficient effort, and if not, how was this handled?

(vi) Illustrate the effectiveness (or lack of it) of the groupwork with evidence from your conference. Include copies of 3 messages that you sent to your group concerning the web channel.

Submitting the assignment
The OU's electronic TMA system only allows you to submit a single file for your TMA, but you will have several. There is a way of combining several files into one, and at the same time compressing them all so they take up much less space. This is called 'zipping', and there are many programs available which zip and unzip files. There is a copy of a zip program called Info-ZIP WiZ included with this course, which should have been put on your hard disk when you installed the course software from the On-Line Applications CD-ROM.
